Background

NSSF developed a 10-year business strategy where it envisions to become “the social security provider of choice”. Meeting that strategy requires among others the development of innovative products to make NSSF a market leader.

NSSF has accordingly developed an innovation policy intended to:

  1. Transform the culture of the Fund to one that is genuinely innovative and performance-driven
  2. Generate a continuous flow of creative and high-quality ideas.
  3. Significantly increase the internal capacity to implement Fund innovations faster and more effectively.
